Our Company

Setting the Scene


Welcome to FEED! We're here to help. Since 2023, our team of eating disorder experts, recovered individuals, and anyone willing to get involved has made great strides in educating the youth about eating disorders.

With FEED, we have traveled to schools around the country, delivered presentations, hosted card drives, hosted events, and authored books.

Though we are proud of our achievements, our pride emerges from the people we have met along the way. Each smile, each "thank you," each new face is what motivates us to work every day, one step closer to recovery all over the world.
